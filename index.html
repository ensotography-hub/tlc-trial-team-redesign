Attorney Tania L. Clark

Personal counsel from a Florida advocate who knows what loss feels like.

My family originated in Pinar del Río, Cuba. When Fidel Castro rose to power, my parents fled to Florida with only hope — and rebuilt their lives in Tampa through sacrifice and quiet work.

That story shaped me. I went into law to bring new hope to others — so that the people who come to us in the worst week of their lives find a firm that treats them like family, not a file number.

For nearly twenty years, our team has fought for the rights of those injured by someone else's negligence. We build relationships rooted in trust. We prepare every case as if it's going to trial.
