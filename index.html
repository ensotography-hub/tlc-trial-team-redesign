Our Process

From the first call to the final wire — the path is clear.

01

Free consultation

You speak directly with Tania or a senior member of the team. We listen, tell you whether you have a case, and what it's likely worth. No pressure, no contract that day.

02

Investigation & treatment

Police reports, witness statements, traffic-cam preservation. We coordinate with your providers and ensure your PIP and MedPay are handled correctly. Your job is to heal.

03

Settlement demand

Once you reach maximum medical improvement, we package the demand: medicals, lost wages, pain and suffering — backed by precedent and ready for litigation.

04

Settle or try

Most cases resolve favorably without trial. The ones that don't, we try — because the carriers know we will. That posture alone changes the offers we receive.
