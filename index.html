Attorney Tania Clark

Personalized, compassionate legal services.

A car accident is a traumatic event that is life altering. In an instant, everything changes.

Victims and their families suddenly have to figure out how to piece their lives back together, knowing it will never be the same. I come from a family who understands what this feels like.

Their story inspired me from a young age to become a lawyer — so that I could bring new hope to others. For nearly twenty years, I've fought for the rights of those injured by someone else's negligence.
