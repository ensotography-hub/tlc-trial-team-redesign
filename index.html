Meet the Attorney

Personal counsel from a Florida advocate who knows what loss feels like.

A car accident is a traumatic event that is life altering. In an instant, everything changes. I come from a family who understands what this feels like.

My family originated in Pinar del Río, Cuba. When Fidel Castro rose to power, my parents fled to Florida with only hope — and rebuilt their lives in Tampa through sacrifice and quiet work. That story shaped me. I went into law to bring new hope to others.

For nearly twenty years, our team has fought for the rights of those injured by someone else's negligence. At TLC Trial Team, we work diligently to help clients achieve justice and fairness.
